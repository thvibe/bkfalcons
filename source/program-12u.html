12U Programs

Ages 11–12 · Bridge to Competitive Baseball

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

About This Program

The 12U program bridges recreational and competitive play, preparing players for travel ball tryouts with more advanced fundamentals and game strategy. Athletes develop stronger mechanics, learn situational baseball, and build the confidence needed to compete at the next level. This is where players begin to take ownership of their development and discover what competitive baseball demands.
