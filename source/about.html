Our Mission

Mission Statement

The Brooklyn Falcons Baseball Organization is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit youth baseball organization located in Brooklyn, New York. The Brooklyn Falcons was established in 2014 by a group of local coaches and parents who wanted to provide a competitive and affordable baseball program for the youth of Brooklyn.

Our mission is to develop young athletes through the game of baseball, focusing on character development, academic excellence, and athletic skill building. We strive to provide a positive environment where players can grow both on and off the field, preparing them for success at the high school, collegiate, and professional levels.

The Falcons offer both recreational and travel baseball programs for players ages 8 through 18, along with academic tutoring, college advisory services, and community outreach. We are committed to making baseball accessible to all families regardless of economic background.
